GReddit is a Chrome extension that converts US Dollars to Reddit Gold. By using Reddit's
reward system as a form of monetary system, that is implemented in the "real" world this
plug-in looks into its implications as a platform that mines people's time, attention and
creativity turning it into a form of useful commodity. You can earn Reddit Gold by posting
great content and approporiate, witty and dank comments in your favorite subreddits.
